Meet the founders

Nitin

Co-Founder · Product Designer & Design Mentor

I didn't plan to be a designer. I planned to be an engineer. NIT Raipur, computer science, the whole thing. But by my second year, I'd already figured out that design was where I actually belonged. So I made the switch and never once looked back.

I've been a potter, an actor, a director, an orator, and somewhere in between all of that, a designer. I think that's actually the point. The more you understand people, the better you design for them. And I've spent a lot of time understanding people.

6+ years across startups in India, UAE and North America. Over a thousand hours mentoring designers across 10 countries. Not because anyone asked me to, just because I find it genuinely hard to keep good things to myself.

At Wormit, I'm the one who walks into the room first. I talk to clients, I read what's not being said, and I make sure we're solving the right problem before we solve any problem at all.

Design, to me, has always been a conversation. I just happen to be very good at those.

Kshamanidhi

Co-Founder · Concept Artist & Product Designer

I don't think design was ever the goal.

As a kid, I took things apart, built random experiments, sketched imaginary worlds and spent more time wondering how things worked than simply using them.

Years later, not much has changed.

The tools are different now. Sketchbooks became Figma. Paper became code. Curiosity found AI, motion, illustration and product design.

I'm still chasing the same feeling.

At Wormit, the sketchbook fills up. The details get caught. The work gets done properly.

The moment an idea stops living in my head and starts existing in the world.

Still thinking,
lets talk,
it won't cost.
