Overview

Finding the right font often means cycling through the same familiar typefaces. The process works — but it's slow, repetitive, and can limit creative exploration.

That's why we built Font Randomiser.

Instead of endlessly browsing font lists, simply select a text layer, choose a few filters — like category, weight, or style — and let the plugin surprise you. Every result stays within your criteria, giving you the freedom to discover without losing control.

Why We Built It

Typography is one of the most important design decisions, yet finding the right typeface can interrupt creative flow.

We wanted font exploration to feel more like discovery than searching. A tool that removes decision fatigue, encourages experimentation, and helps designers stumble upon combinations they might never have considered.

Sometimes the right font isn't the obvious choice — it's the unexpected one.

How It Works
01 Select Choose any text layer in your Figma file.
02 Filter Refine the results by category, weight, or style.
03 Randomise Generate a font that matches your preferences.
04 Explore Keep going until something feels right.
Designed for Exploration
  • Break free from familiar font choices
  • Discover typefaces you might have overlooked
  • Explore ideas faster with less manual searching
  • Stay focused on designing, not browsing

Less searching. More discovering.

Built right

We built Font Randomiser to make typography exploration faster, more enjoyable, and a little more unexpected. Sometimes, all it takes is one random font to unlock a completely new direction.
